Sandokan Enduro

Bikulture and Joe Cycles present a Mountain Bike Enduro race in Madeira

[EN] On Sunday the 15th of December takes place the Avalanche Raposeira, an already well-established mass start downhill race, always a very popular event in Madeira.

Bikulture Madeira and Joe Cycles have decided to turn it into a whole weekend of celebrating Mountain Bike by organizing on the previous day a special race, the Sandokan Enduro.

Our very own vision of Enduro racing, with a limited group of 30 elite riders, and the best singletracks in our mountains backyard (including newly developed trails).

But also an introduction to Enduro for less experienced riders, as they will have the opportunity to ride these trails as a additional guided group, once all the racers have departed, in a more 'relaxed', non-timed mode. We call it the Sandokan experience!

Topped by an after-party at our shop, with food, drinks, music, videos, and prizes!

[PT] Domingo dia 15 de Dezembro, realiza-se a muito esperada Avalanche da Raposeira, justamente apelidada de corrida do ano!

A Bikulture e Joe Cycles, esperam transformar este grande evento num fim de semana épico de BTT. Organizando no dia anterior o Sandokan Enduro.

Uma corrida de Enduro, limitada a 30 competidores de elite, nos melhores singletracks que as montanhas dos Prazeres têm para oferecer. Incluindo dois novos percursos.

Como introdução ao enduro, decorrerá em paralelo a Sandokan Experience. Num formato mais relaxado, sem "tempos" e com as ligações feitas em transportes.

A cereja no topo do bolo, será a after-party no quartel general da Bikulture.
